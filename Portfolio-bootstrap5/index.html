About Me

My name is Eko Firlianto used to be called Eko. My height was 182 cm and it was quite annoying for me because at school I was always the highest person in class or even at school. I am not comfortable with this because every time I walk people always see me strangely. Sometimes I'm confused whether they see me too high or I'm too handsome, but it looks like I'm too handsome.

I really like photography the last few years, I started to be interested in the world of photography when I was in the 3rd semester of college, even though I don't have a digital camera, I still often take pictures even though I only use a smartphone, usually I often take pictures of beaches in Bangka Belitung because there are lots of great photo spots on the beach, especially around Sungailiat.
